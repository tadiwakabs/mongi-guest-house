1. How to Delete Your Account

Users can permanently delete their account directly inside the app by following these steps:

    • • Open Mongi Guest House app
    • • Go to Settings
    • • Select Delete Account
    • • Confirm deletion when prompted

Once confirmed, the deletion process begins immediately.

2. What Data Is Deleted

When you delete your account, the following data is permanently removed from our systems:

  • • User account (including email + login credentials)
  • • Profile information
  • • Any app-generated content (bookings, transactions, activity, etc.)
  • • Rewards balance
3. Data Retention

Mongi Guest House does not retain any personal data after account deletion.
