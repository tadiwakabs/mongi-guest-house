Amenities

At Mongi Guest House, every stay is designed for comfort, style, and convenience. Choose from a range of beautifully appointed ensuite rooms to suit your needs: from cozy Economy and Standard rooms to spacious Superior, Deluxe, and Junior Suites. Each room blends modern design with homely touches to create the perfect retreat after a day in Harare.

Every ensuite includes a variety of thoughtful features, such as:

  • Flat Screen Smart TV with Digital Satellite Television (DSTV)
  • Unlimited High Speed Wi-Fi (Fiber)
  • Mini Fridge (available in all rooms except Economy & Standard Double)
  • Housekeeping Service Included
  • Hotel Quality Bed & Linens
  • Private Bathroom with Walk-In Shower & Complementary Toiletries
  • Tub Sofa and Electric Kettle for comfort and convenience
  • Lamps, Portable Fans, USB Charging Port, and Full-Size Mirror
  • Desk & Chair, Cabinet Drawer, and Nightstand (except Standard Ensuites)
